About RocCon

Rochester's Comic and Pop Culture Convention

RocCon is Rochester's version of the hugely popular conventions held in major cities around the globe. Our convention is a family friendly event, featuring guests from the fandoms of comic books, sci-fi, anime, and gaming (including TCG). RocCon is the creation of a longtime fan of everything pop culture, who realized in 2009 that Rochester didn't have a "comic-con". RocCon was established in 2010, and we've held the convention annually each September ever since.

All of ours rooms are filled with all kinds of programming. You'll get a program when you arrive. We also put the program up shortly before each year's convention so you can plan ahead for what you'd like to do, who you want to see, and when to hit the food truck rodeo. Plan ahead so that you don't miss a moment of the cosplay contest in the big theatre!

Each room changes programming just about every hour! You'll find all our special guests doing panels as well! That includes all our guests, actors, artists, authors, cosplayers, and a variety of other people who come in to do programming for the event. In addition, the food truck rodeo is legendary! When you've worked up an appetite, head out to the side of the venue for choices from pretty much every continent!

At RocCon, there's something for everyone. We have: four floors of fandom fun, two big dealers rooms, a cosplay contest with prizes each day of the show, four event rooms, and a huge theatre for our main events! Programming covers all ages and interests: drawing, writing, superheroes, sci-fi, comic books, gaming, and anime. Everyone is invited to come play in the RocCon Sandbox! The show is appropriate for ages six and over. You'll get to meet well-known, wonderful guests from all the different genres of the convention and enjoy a wide variety of artists, authors, and vendors of all kinds!
